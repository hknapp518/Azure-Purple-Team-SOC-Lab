Security groups were created to support organizational role separation and simplify access governance across enterprise resources.

Example governance groups included:

- Clinical Users
- Clinical Data Team
- Research Leads
- Security & Compliance
- IT
- External

This structure supports least privilege access management and improves administrative consistency across Microsoft 365 and Azure environments.
